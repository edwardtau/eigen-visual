What to notice

Use the visuals first. Let the algebra explain what your eye found.

Core question

Does the image of v stay on the dashed gold line? If yes, that direction is acting like an eigenvector. If the image falls away from that line, the transformation is mixing directions.

How lambda appears

When the drift drops to zero, the along-probe scale becomes the corresponding eigenvalue. Positive values keep direction, negative values flip through the origin.

Suggested route

Start with two real directions, then try the flip and the shear. Finish with pure rotation to see a matrix whose invariant lines disappear from the real plane.
